Introduction

Introduction The God created us and given us five senses, but our connections to computers are still “sensory deprived and physically limited”. Have you ever gone frustrated or bored by the traditional way of computing that is by keyboard and mouse? If yes then this is the workshop you are looking for. This workshop aims to give you an opportunity to explore surface and gesture computing along with hands on experience of the same. It will take you to the new level of computing with the following learning outcomes:
-> Convert your normal laptop or desktop into multi-touch screen device.
-> Multi-touch Projector.
-> Make your own multi-touch pad.
-> Control your laptop through hand gestures by using Microsoft Kinect.
-> Control your mouse cursor by voice.
-> Play virtual drum.
-> Make your own wireless measuring tape.
-> Capture 3D images through videos.
-> Create your own augmented world and play with it!!


Details

  1.   Date: 23-25th December 2016
  2.   Duration: 3 Day [8 hrs per day]
  3.   Venue: IIT Jodhpur
  4.   Cost of workshop: Rs 1500/- per participant

Introduction

This workshop will give you a unique opportunity to experience a hands-on application of a Civil Engineering Mini Project. Includes Practical sessions on CAD based Bridge Design and Testing. Participants will gain thorough knowledge of various technical concepts involved in Civil Engineering. Helps in making the best innovative bridges with designing and fabrication. Finally, Bridge Design Contest is conducted to provide the participants a real-life introduction to Civil Engineering. Faculty will discuss with students how to implement the gained knowledge for projects in future sharing innovative ideas. Bridges will be fabricated and tested for maximum stress and load.
